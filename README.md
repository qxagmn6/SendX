## SendX Reviews

SendX has received mixed feedback from users across various review platforms. The general consensus reveals a platform that excels in affordability and ease of use, though experiences with deliverability and support vary.

### What Users Love About SendX

**Affordability and Value**: Multiple reviewers consistently praise SendX's pricing model. Users appreciate the unlimited email sends and subscriber-based pricing, especially as their contact lists grow. One marketing manager noted that SendX became significantly more cost-effective than MailerLite once they reached 15,000 contacts.

**Ease of Use**: The intuitive interface receives frequent compliments. Users describe the platform as easy to learn with minimal training required. One reviewer mentioned that setting up drip campaigns and importing contacts was extremely straightforward, putting SendX at the top of their priority list.

**Customer Support**: Many users report positive experiences with the support team. Reviews mention fast response times, helpful guidance, and genuine care for customer success. One user specifically praised support staff member Najwa for going the extra mile to resolve issues quickly.

**Feature Set**: Users appreciate having access to all features regardless of plan tier. The drag-and-drop editor, automation workflows, forms, landing pages, and analytics are frequently mentioned as valuable tools that justify the investment.

### Common Concerns from Users

**Deliverability Issues**: Some users report problems with email delivery rates. A few reviewers experienced emails not reaching recipients or being delayed significantly. One user mentioned delivery rates as low as 20%, though it's worth noting that deliverability can be affected by many factors including sender reputation and list quality.

**Support Response Times**: While many praise the support team, others express frustration with delayed responses due to time zone differences (the team is based in India). Some users reported waiting days for responses when they needed immediate assistance.

**Automation Complexity**: Several reviewers note that while basic automation is straightforward, setting up complex conditional workflows can become confusing. The lack of pre-built automation templates (compared to competitors offering hundreds) is mentioned as a limitation.

**Learning Curve for Advanced Features**: Some users find that while basic features are intuitive, more advanced functionality requires additional time to master fully.
